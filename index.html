How we met

In the early spring of March 2015, I found myself amidst the vibrant college atmosphere, embarking on a new club venture with my friends. It was during the preparations for an upcoming event that our paths crossed. On that particular day, she joined the club, and my initial impressions of her were quite neutral, as I approached her with a professional demeanor for club-related activities. As time passed, I started inviting her to attend future events. However, it seemed like she would fly away whenever she noticed my presence nearby, leading to moments of uncertainty. It wasn't until after we graduated that our relationship took a turn. The turning point came when she got an Android phone and installed WhatsApp. With this newfound communication channel, we found ourselves drawn closer to each other. Looking back, that college club was not only the beginning of a beautiful journey, but it also brought us together in a way that I could never have anticipated. Our story is one of chance encounters, hesitant steps, and the magic of technology that eventually led us to the love we share today.
